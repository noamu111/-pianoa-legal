Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 13, 2025

1. Introduction

PIANOA ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our App with TikTok integration, we may collect:

  • TikTok Profile Information: Your TikTok username, display name, and avatar (via user.info.basic scope)
  • Authorization Tokens: Tokens required to post content on your behalf

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Post piano tutorial videos to your TikTok account (with your permission)
  • Display your profile information within the App
  • Provide and improve our services

4. TikTok Data

Our App uses TikTok's Login Kit and Content Posting API. We access only the data you authorize through TikTok's permission system. We use the following TikTok scopes:

  • user.info.basic: To identify your account
  • video.upload: To upload videos to your account
  • video.publish: To publish videos directly

5. Data Storage

We store minimal data required for the App to function. Authorization tokens are stored securely and are only used to post content on your behalf.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except as required to provide our services (e.g., posting to TikTok).

7. Your Rights

You can revoke the App's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's settings. You may also contact us to request deletion of any data we have stored.

8. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

9. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our YouTube channel: PIANOA on YouTube
