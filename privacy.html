1. Introduction

PIANOA ("we", "our", or "the Service") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

When you use PIANOA, we may collect:

  • TikTok Account Information: Basic profile information (username, display name) when you authorize the app
  • Authorization Tokens: Secure tokens that allow us to upload videos on your behalf

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect or store:

  • Your TikTok password
  • Your personal videos or content (we only process videos you explicitly select)
  • Your followers, following lists, or engagement data
  • Your messages or comments
  • Any financial information

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Upload piano tutorial clips to your TikTok account
  • Display your username to confirm you're logged in

5. Data Storage and Security

Your authorization tokens are stored securely on your local device. We do not store your credentials on any external servers. All communication with TikTok uses secure HTTPS connections.

6. Third-Party Services

PIANOA integrates with:

  • TikTok: To upload videos to your profile. TikTok's privacy policy applies to your TikTok account.
  • YouTube: To access your public piano tutorial videos (only videos you own).

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke PIANOA's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok settings
  • Request deletion of any stored data
  • Know what data we have about you

8. Data Retention

We retain authorization tokens only while you actively use the Service. If you revoke access or uninstall the application, all local data is removed.

9. Children's Privacy

PIANOA is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our YouTube channel: @pianoa-6448
